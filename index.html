the birds must be free

Between 0.4 and 2.1 million birds are killed and illegally captured each year in northern and central Europe and the Caucasus region, according to the report. The organization denounces that they are shot or captured with stocks or glue traps for sport, consumption or tradition. According to research, 66% of the 457 native species on the European continent are affected by these practices....

Claudio Paredes - 2024
